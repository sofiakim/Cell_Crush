3) This is a platelet. A platelet is small cytoplasmic body in the blood that aids in clotting, blood platelet.

4) This is a red blood cell. A red blood cell contains hemoglobin which transports oxygen.

5) This is a muscle cell. Muscle cells create ATP which powers human body.

6) This is a white blood cell. A white blood cell acts as an immune system.

7) This is a cancer cell. A cancer cell increases and is able to cover human body, which leads them to death.

8) This is a virus. A virus is the one that makes human sick.

9) This is a fat cell. A fat cell is large in volume and increases the risk of getting diseases.
